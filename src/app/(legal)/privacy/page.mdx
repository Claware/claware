# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 7, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Claware ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website (claware.ai) and our automated deployment services.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

### Account Information
- Name, email address, and profile information from Google sign-in
- Account creation and login timestamps

### Service Information
- AI model preferences selected during setup
- Messaging channel configurations (Telegram bot tokens)
- Deployment preferences and settings

### Payment Information
- Payment details processed securely through Stripe
- Billing history and subscription status
- **We do not store your credit card information on our servers**

### Analytics Data
- Anonymous usage data through Vercel Analytics
- Page views, session duration, and traffic sources
- Error logs for debugging purposes

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provision and deploy your OpenClaw instance
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Provide technical support and troubleshooting
- Improve our services and user experience
- Communicate important service updates

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.**

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

- **Encryption:** All data in transit uses TLS/SSL encryption
- **Access Control:** Strict internal access controls and authentication
- **No Storage of Secrets:** Bot tokens are used only during deployment and not retained
- **Regular Audits:** Security practices are regularly reviewed

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Payment records:** Retained as required by tax laws (typically 7 years)
- **Analytics data:** Anonymized after 26 months
- **Deleted accounts:** Personal data is removed within 30 days of account deletion

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Delete** your personal data (with certain exceptions)

To exercise these rights, email us at [support@claware.ai](mailto:support@claware.ai).

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies:

- **Essential cookies:** Required for authentication and core functionality
- **Analytics cookies:** Anonymous usage tracking via Vercel Analytics (no advertising cookies)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

## 10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, consistent with this Privacy Policy.

- Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
